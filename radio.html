Shortwave Radio

Several international news organizations are broadcasting their programming on the shortwave radio bands. If you have a shortwave radio, you can listen to programs from many countries around the world.

Here are some frequencies and times for shortwave programming from a few major news outlets (all times in UTC):
Freq. (kHz) Time (UTC) Org. Program Info
15,735 13:00 - 15:00 BBC News
5,875 20:00-22:00 BBC News
15,770 19:30-20:00 M-F VOA Flashpoint Ukraine (Eng)
7,730 02:00-03:00, 04:00-05:00 Radio Liberty News (Russian)
7,730 05:00 - 06:00 Radio Ukraine Intl. Various other times on 7,730 and 15,770

If you have Internet access, you can find a nearly complete list of all known stations on short-wave.info.
